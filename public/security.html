Platform Security

Your business trust is our top priority. We employ multi-layered security to keep your deals and data safe.

Our Security Philosophy

At NexTrade, we understand that B2B commerce involves sensitive data—from GST numbers to proprietary pricing. Our security framework is designed to protect every interaction on the platform.

Data Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is protected using 256-bit SSL encryption. Your passwords and private deal details are hashed and encrypted at rest.

Wholesaler Verification

To maintain a safe marketplace, we hand-verify business documents (GST/Trade Licenses) for all wholesalers. This multi-step audit ensures you are only dealing with legitimate business entities.

Secure Negotiations

Our negotiation system and AI consultant, Nexa, operate within a sandboxed environment. Your chat history and deal status are only visible to the parties involved in the transaction.

Reporting Vulnerabilities

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability on NexTrade, please report it to our security team immediately at security@nextrade.com. We take all reports seriously and investigate them promptly.
