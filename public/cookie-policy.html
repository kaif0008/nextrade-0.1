Cookie Policy

How we use cookies to improve your marketplace experience and optimize platform performance.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide a better, faster, and safer experience. They allow our system (and Nexa) to remember your preferences and keep your session secure while you negotiate deals.

How NexTrade Uses Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for you to log in, access your dashboard, and use the message portal.
  • Performance Cookies: These help us understand how users interact with our pages, which categories are popular, and how we can improve platform speed.
  • Personalization Cookies: Used by Nexa to remember your recent searches and provide better product recommendations.

Managing Your Cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. However, if you disable essential cookies, many features of the NexTrade platform (including deal management and the AI consultant) may not function correctly.

Updates to This Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or data protection laws. Please check this page regularly for the latest information.
