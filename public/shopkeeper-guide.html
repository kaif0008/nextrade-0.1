Shopkeeper Guide

Your blueprint for sourcing products at the best bulk prices through the NexTrade network.

Sourcing Like a Pro

1

Search & Explore

Use the "Explore Products" page to find specific items or browse by categories. You can also view Wholesalers directly to see their entire catalog.

2

Post a Requirement

Can't find exactly what you need? Go to the Retailer Dashboard and post a "Requirement". Wholesalers will see your request and send you offers directly.

3

Negotiate the Deal

Found a product? Start a "Deal". You can propose a price based on your quantity needs. The wholesaler can then accept your price or make a counter-offer.

Using the Message Portal

Every deal has a private conversation thread. Use this to:

  • Request custom samples or detailed photos.
  • Discuss shipping costs and estimated delivery times.
  • Finalize packaging or branding requirements.

Mastering the Market with Nexa

Nexa is your personal assistant. Retailers who succeed on NexTrade use Nexa to:

  • Monitor Status: "Nexa, what is the status of my electronics deal?"
  • Discovery: "Nexa, show me available products for summer inventory."
  • Analysis: Nexa keeps your business organized by summarizing your active negotiations.
