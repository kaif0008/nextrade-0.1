Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about the NexTrade marketplace.

General Questions

Is NexTrade free to use?

NexTrade is free to join for both Wholesalers and Retailers. We currently do not charge listing fees. Premium featured placements may be introduced in the future.

Who is Nexa?

Nexa is our proprietary AI business consultant. She helps users manage their deals, find products, and analyze market demand in real-time. You can find her in the bottom corner of every page.

For Wholesalers

How do I become a verified wholesaler?

To become verified, ensure your profile is 100% complete, including a valid GST number and business address. Verification is done automatically by our system or manually by our audit team within 48 hours.

Can I update my product prices after an inquiry?

Yes. The NexTrade platforms supports a dynamic negotiation system. You can update the "Offered Price" for a specific deal based on the quantity requested by the retailer.

For Retailers

What is a "Requirement"?

A Requirement is a request you post when you can't find a specific product. Wholesalers who stock that category will see your requirement and can send you direct offers.

How do I know if a wholesaler is trustworthy?

Look for the "Verified" badge on wholesaler profiles. You can also check their rating and deal history to see how they have performed with other retailers.
